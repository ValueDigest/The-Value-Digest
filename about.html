What is The Value Digest?

The Value Digest is an independent ASX research publication focused on identifying Australian-listed companies trading below their intrinsic value. Founded in 2021, it applies a disciplined, fundamentals-first framework to cut through market noise and focus on what actually matters: real profits, sensible valuations, and downside protection.

The philosophy

The approach is grounded in the principles of value investing — buying good businesses at fair prices, rather than speculating on sentiment or momentum. Every stock idea must pass six quantitative criteria covering earnings quality, profitability, dividend consistency, debt levels, and valuation before it is considered for the portfolio or published as a pitch.


The research is deliberately backward-looking and evidence-based. Rather than forecasting earnings or predicting macro events, the focus is on companies with a long, verifiable track record — letting the numbers speak for themselves.

Who is it for?

The Value Digest is written for self-directed Australian investors who want clear, honest research without the noise. Whether you're building a long-term portfolio, looking for a second opinion on a stock, or just starting out — the goal is to make rigorous fundamental analysis accessible to everyone.

Transparency and disclosure

The Value Digest does not hold an Australian Financial Services Licence (AFSL). All content is general information only and does not constitute financial advice. Where the author holds a position in a stock discussed, this is clearly disclosed within the relevant publication. The site does not accept payment for coverage of any company.
