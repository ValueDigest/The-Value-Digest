We may collect the following types of information:

  • Contact form submissions — your name, email address, and message content when you contact us via the website.
  • Newsletter sign-ups — your name and email address when you subscribe to the free newsletter.
  • Usage data — standard web server logs and analytics data, including pages visited, browser type, device type, and referring URL. This data is collected in aggregate and is not linked to individual identities.

How we use your information

Information you provide is used only for the purpose it was submitted:

Cookies and advertising

This website uses cookies. Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. We use cookies for the following purposes:

  • Analytics — we may use cookies to understand how visitors use the site in aggregate, to improve its content and structure.

Most web browsers allow you to control or disable cookies through browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect the functionality of some features of this website.
